2. Enable Unknown Sources

Android 8.0+ requires per-app permission:

  1. Open Settings ➔ Apps & notifications ➔ See all apps, then choose your browser (e.g. Chrome).
  2. Tap Advanced ➔ Install unknown apps.
  3. Toggle Allow from this source to On.
  4. Return to your browser’s download list and tap the APK.

On Android 7.x and below: Settings ➔ Security ➔ Unknown sources, then enable installation from unknown sources.

3. Install the App

  1. Tap Install when prompted, review permissions, and accept.
  2. Once complete, tap Open.

4. Grant Location Permissions

On first launch, the app will request permission to access your device’s GPS:

  • Select Allow only while using the app or Allow all the time for continuous background tracking.
  • If GPS is off, you’ll be prompted to enable it. Swipe down the notification shade or go to Settings ➔ Location and toggle On.

This allows Prayer Walk Ireland to accurately track your route, distance, and duration.

5. Start Your First Prayer Walk

  1. From the main menu, tap Prayer Walk.
  2. Wait for the map and your current location marker to appear.
  3. Tap the Start button. Optionally enter a label for your walk.
  4. Monitor elapsed time, distance, and average speed in the top panel.
  5. You can Pause, Resume, or Stop at any time.
  6. Confirm Stop to save your session; your route will be plotted on the map and stored.

6. Troubleshooting & Tips

  • Download fails? Ensure your link uses ?dl=1 on Dropbox or proper query param for Google Drive/MEGA.
  • Cannot install? Verify your unknown-sources setting.
  • GPS issues? Move to an open area and enable Wi‑Fi scanning under Settings ➔ Location.
  • App crashes? Reinstall from the download link and grant all permissions.

Need more help? Visit our Help & Support page or email prayerwalkirl@gmail.com.
